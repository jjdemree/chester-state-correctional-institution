Bibliography
Census of State and Federal Adult Correctional Facilities Series

This bibliography includes published and unpublished works that are
related to ICPSR series 67. It includes works that are based on
primary or secondary analysis of the data, or which describe or critique
those data or the collection methodology. This list represents all items
known to ICPSR as of 2007-12-07.

If you publish a work or know of other works that are based on these data,
please send the complete citation and name of the ICPSR series used to:
bibliography@icpsr.umich.edu.


1. Geter, Leon T., "Re-entry statistics now available on BJS web site."
Corrections Today. Aug 2003, 65, (5), 66 - 67.

2. Kubrin, Charis E.; Stewart, Eric A., "Predicting Who Reoffends: The
Neglected Role of Neighborhood Context in Recidivism Studies." Criminology.
Feb 2006, 44, (1), 165 - 197.

3. Petersilia, Joan, "Parole and prisoner reentry in the United States." In
Tonry, Michael; Petersilia, Joan, Prisons. Crime and Justice: A Review of
Research. 479 - 529, Chicago, IL: University of Chicago Press,1999.

4. Petersilia, Joan, "Probation in the United States." In Tonry, Michael,
Crime and Justice: A Review of Research. 149 - 200, Chicago, IL: University
of Chicago Press,1997.

5. Reuter, Peter, "Why can't we make prohibition work better?  Some
consequences of ignorning the unattractive." In Perspectives on Crime and
Justice Lecture Series,. 23 - 45, Washington, DC: United States Department
of Justice, National Institute of Justice,1997. NCJ 166609
